Who We Are

We created "Where You Are" to bridge the gap between the Christian Church and the average American man. We do not seek to replace the local church, and we are not licensed counselors. We are faithful Christian men who desire to apply Biblical wisdom in our lives, and a passion to share with others.


The founder began his Christian faith as a disciple in the "Navigators". While not a professionally trained theologian, he has spent countless hours researching the Bible, the Christian faith, Christian Church history, apologetics, and various other related topics. Along with theological knowledge, he spent 20 years as a US Army officer, and is well versed in the art of leadership and the principles of training and mentoring. As a former atheist and life-long introvert, he understands the barriers that exist between a man and faith.


A statement of faith is available upon request.
