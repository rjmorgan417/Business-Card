About Us

We are a children's and maternity clothing store that specializes in New and High Quality Preowned clothing, furniture and accessories.


We have a unique Exchange program that allows you in-store credit for your High Quality clothing that you wish to trade with us. We also pay CASH for furniture items including cribs, changing tables, bassinets, strollers, swings and car seats too! Condition is everything and we do check all items for any recalls that have been announced.
